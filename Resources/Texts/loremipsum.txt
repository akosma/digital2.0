Liquid contents

The approach was organised in such a way as to differentiate the contents according to the position of the device (horizontal or vertical) as initiated with the iPhone; but the iPad made this approach essential. Indeed, it is unthinkable to design an application without producing a model specific to each of the two positions.

In use, we notice that not only the layout of the various contents (texts, images, films, background) must be arranged specifically, but that each of the two positions makes it possible to highlight certain types of content (text or image)
